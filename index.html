Philanthropic Yield Protocol (PYP) & Decentralised Finance.

Project ONE: - The first protocol/product and primary focus of SubsidiumDev.

Subsidium is focused on bringing fullproof philanthropic solutions to Ethereum based decentralised finance platforms. Across our social media's you may come across the term "Project ONE", POne refers to our initial protocol concept known as the "Philanthropic Yield Protocol" (PYP). This protocol focuses on allowing individual "yield farmers" or "stakers" across different platforms to delegate a percentage of their earnings to charitable causes. The way in which this will be achieved is through governance within a DAO framework, SubsidiumDAO will be formed with the criteria of managing multiple vaults that correspond to different Subsidium protocols. The unified objective of governance is achieving the highest return on donated assets as to:

  1. donate a larger, uniform amount to verified charities.
  2. distribute through ledger based accounting a higher tax write-off yield than original donation.
