Subsidium Digital

Subsidium Digital is a cooperative aiming to provide to the ever growing cryptocurrency community.

Founded with Charity in mind

The CryptoCurrency market has long been purely based on speculation regarding platforms and their futures, along with said market comes greed. Having spent a good portion of time as a retail investors in crypto and traditional markets, Subsidium came together to bring a much needed, less greed focused product. Volatility is the enemy to stable investment plans, Subsidium is on track to develop partnerships with Crypto Indices and their respective yielding pools to suggest our Nominal Charity Pool (NCP) Token. This token is aimed to delegate a portion of vault yields to charity, with its utility being a governance model of donation.

Who we are & What we stand for -

Unlike the many decentralised finance protocols that have been released over the past few years subsidium is very different.

Subsidium was a theory by a young, passionate team of Computer Scientist's who's aims were to collaborate on a project that would bring less collaborative greed and more peer philanthropy. The ability for individuals to yield farm with different protocols has grown at an alarming rate and has shown a high rate of greed, with liquidity providers chasing the highest APY%'s. For the general public investing in crypto alone is daunting and we are compassionate to those who have entered the market expecting large yields only to be parted with their hard earned finances in the case of a liquidity pull.

We stand for Decentralised Financial Freedom for the masses, and we plan to achieve that by utilising crypto indices. Crypto indices offer a level of investment hedge not seen in any other sector of cryptocurrency. Index Co-operative and their basis $DPI token has long been an inspiration to the team.

Our primary objective is to introduce the Nominal Charitable Pool (NCP) Token concept to yield vaults whereby the users can opt to dedicate a small portion of their APY% to SubsidiumDAO.
